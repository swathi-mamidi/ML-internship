Our Mission

At Communicable Diseases Information, our mission is to provide accurate and up-to-date information about communicable diseases, particularly COVID-19 and tuberculosis. We aim to raise awareness, promote prevention, and support individuals and communities in their fight against these diseases.

COVID-19

A person's likelihood of being diagnosed with COVID-19 can be predicted by entering their symptoms into a form utilizing a machine learning algorithm. This advanced technology analyzes the provided symptoms and provides an assessment, helping determine whether the individual is potentially affected by the virus or not. By leveraging the power of machine learning, this predictive system enhances the accuracy and efficiency of diagnosing COVID-19 based on symptom data.

Our Team

Our dedicated team at Communicable Diseases Information is composed of experts in the field of infectious diseases and public health. With a deep understanding of COVID-19, tuberculosis, and other communicable diseases, our team works tirelessly to gather, curate, and present accurate information to empower individuals and communities in their efforts to combat these diseases.
