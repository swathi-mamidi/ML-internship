General Information on COVID-19

COVID-19 is a viral illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It can cause a range of symptoms, including fever, cough, shortness of breath, fatigue, and loss of taste or smell. It primarily spreads through respiratory droplets when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or talks.

COVID-19 Prevention Measures

To reduce the risk of COVID-19 transmission, it is important to:

  • Wear a mask in public settings
  • Practice frequent hand hygiene by washing hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds
  • Maintain a physical distance of at least 6 feet from others
  • Follow local health guidelines and regulations
